1. Scope of Work
1.1 Project Overview The Service Provider agrees to develop a comprehensive software solution, the Garage Web App, to streamline and enhance the day-to-day workflow of Abe's Garage. The application will include public-facing static pages, employee registration and authentication, customer management, order management, and private pages accessible only by authenticated employees.

1.2 Functional Requirements The Service Provider shall develop the following functionalities as part of the Garage Web App:
Public-Facing Static Pages: Home Page, About Us Page, Services Page, and Contact Us Page.
Employee Registration and Authentication.
Customer Management.
Order Management.
Private Pages only accessible by authenticated employees.

1.3 Non-functional Requirements The Service Provider shall ensure the following non-functional requirements are met:
Security: Implement robust security measures to protect sensitive data.
Performance: Design and optimize the application for acceptable response times.
Scalability: Design the application to easily accommodate potential growth.
Usability: Provide an intuitive and user-friendly interface.
Maintainability: Ensure the application is well-documented and easily maintainable.
Reliability: Design the application to minimize the risk of system failure and data loss.

1.4 Technology Stack The Service Provider shall utilize the following technology stack:
Backend: Node.js, Express.js, MySQL.
Frontend: React.js, Bootstrap.
Deployment and Version Control: AWS, Git (hosted on GitHub).

1.5 Project Timeline The Parties agree to the following project timeline:
Project Kickoff and Planning (1 week)
